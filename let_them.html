Know Your Worth

Let Them

Let them lose you if they think you are not enough.

If they don’t see your value, let them walk.

People make time for who they care about.

If they are not making time for you, then believe them when they say they are too busy.

Don’t Force It

Don’t force it.

If they wanted to be with you, they would be.

If they choose someone or something over you, let them.

It hurts, but you can’t make someone feel what they don’t.

Remember Your Worth

You shouldn’t beg for someone to care, to love, to put in the effort.

If they are okay with going weeks without you, let them.

Focus on those who see your worth without you having to prove it.

At the end of the day, remember your worth.

Don’t settle for less than what you deserve.

Keep your standards high and your circle tight.

The right people will value you and give you time.
